Hojicha

Hojicha is a type of green tea that has been roasted, thus giving it a warmer, nuttier taste. It can be enjoyed in various ways.

How can hojicha be used?

  • As plain hot tea
  • Iced or hot latte with milk of choice
  • In desserts like tiramisu
  • As soft serve or ice cream flavors
  • Basically anything you can think of!
